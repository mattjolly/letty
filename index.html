Letty is one of the UK’s most exciting and versatile French horn players, with her career spanning across a variety of genres.

Whether performing as a soloist, orchestral and chamber musician or as an educator, Letty's energy and enthusiasm have driven her work across a huge range of musical styles, forming dynamic collaborations in the UK and worldwide.

Performance

Letty has worked with many of the UK's leading orchestras and opera companies and has performed and toured with a huge variety of artists.

She has recorded as a session musician for numerous TV and film scores and has performed globally on a range of diverse projects.

Education

Letty has always believed that music performance and education go hand in hand and throughout her career, she has aimed to reach as many children and young people as possible.

She has demonstrated the French horn to quite literally thousands of children and is happiest when sharing her love of music to new audiences.
