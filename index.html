Performance

As an orchestral and chamber musician, Letty has worked with orchestras including BBC Concert Orchestra, Britten Sinfonia, National Symphony Orchestra, City of London Sinfonia, Royal Philharmonic Orchestra and English National Ballet, as well with English National Opera and English Touring Opera.

Letty’s playing has been broadcast on BBC Radio 3 numerous times, including most recently with the Goldfield Ensemble on BBCR3's Hear and Now as well as In Tune programmes.

Theatre

Letty has considerable experience working in theatre and performed onstage for the highly acclaimed productions of Amadeus and Macbeth at the National Theatre (both as an original cast member and for the production’s revival, as well as for the NT Live broadcast).

“These 21 musicians are the backbone of this production, scattering and re-forming throughout, like some sort of murmuration of musically gifted starlings.”
The Times

Letty has also performed in Julius Caesar as part the Royal Shakespeare Company's Rome season in Stratford-upon-Avon.

Education

Letty has always believed that music performance and education go hand in hand and throughout her career, she has aimed to reach as many children and young people as possible.

She has demonstrated the French horn to quite literally thousands of children and is happiest when sharing her love of music to new audiences.
