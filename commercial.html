Commercial Performance

Letty has collaborated with commercial artists including Badly Drawn Boy, Charlotte Church, Kate Tempest, Laura Mvula, Sam Lee, Squarepusher and Shirley Bassey, and has toured with artists including Tony Hadley/Spandau Ballet, ABC and The Slow Show, as well as several arena tours for projects including The Sessions (UK/Europe), Il Divo (UK) and Ajay Atul (India).

Festivals

Letty has performed at festivals ranging from the Oxford Contemporary Music and Cheltenham Literary Festivals in the UK, to the Sud-Tirol Jazz and Haizetara Festivals in Europe; as well as at high-profile festivals including the Edinburgh Festival and Glastonbury.
