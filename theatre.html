Theatre

Letty has considerable experience working in theatre and performed onstage for the highly acclaimed production of Amadeus at the National Theatre (both as an original cast member and for the production’s revival, as well as for the NT Live broadcast).

“The National Theatre’s Amadeus is note-perfect”, The Telegraph

Letty then went on to perform in the National Theatre’s production of Macbeth, starring Rory Kinnear and Anne-Marie Duff (including NT live broadcast to cinemas worldwide).

Working with composer Orlando Gough, musical director Marc Tritschler and instrument-maker Simon Allen, the production involved an incredible R&D opportunity to make new instruments that would feature in the play’s post-apocalyptic setting. As one of only two musicians in the production, Letty was involved with the creation of these new instruments as well as being part of the composition process.

Letty has worked with the Royal Shakespeare Company, performing in Julius Caesar as part of the RSC’s Rome Season. The score was composed by Mira Calix for live brass instruments with electronics.

Finally, Letty has performed for theatre productions including Little Miss Sunshine (Arcola Theatre, London), and Disney's The Lion King.
